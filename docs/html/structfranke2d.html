Franke's bivariate function.

Franke's bivariate function is a weighted sum of four exponential

\[ \begin{aligned} \nonumber f(x) &= 0.75 e^{\left(-\frac{(9x_1-2)^2}{4} - \frac{(9x_2-2)^2}{4} \right)} \\ \nonumber &+ 0.75 e^{\left(-\frac{(9x_1+1)^2}{49} - \frac{(9x_2+1)}{10} \right)} \\ \nonumber &+ 0.5 e^{\left(-\frac{(9x_1-7)^2}{4} - \frac{(9x_2-3)^2}{4} \right)} \\ \nonumber &- 0.2 e^{\left(-(9x_1-4)^2 - (9x_2-7)^2 \right)} \end{aligned} \]
