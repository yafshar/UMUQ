TEST ( densityFunction_test  ,
HandlesMultinomialDistributionConstruction   
)

Test to check multinomialDistribution

Example reference:
http://www.probabilityformula.org/multinomial-probability.html

First example

This is an experiment of drawing a random card from an ordinary playing cards deck is done with replacing it back. This was done ten times. Find the probability of getting 2 spades, 3 diamond, 3 club and 2 hearts.

Second example

In case of 10 bits, what is the probability that 5 are excellent, 2 are good and 2 are fair and 1 is poor? Classification of individual bits are independent events and that the probabilities of A, B, C and D are 40%, 20%, 5% and 1% respectively.

Note
The multinomialDistribution would normalize the probabilities of A, B, C and D to {40/66, 20/66, 5/66, 1/66}.
