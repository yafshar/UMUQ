Detailed Description

template<typename T>
class umuq::latinHypercubeSampling< T >

This class generates sample points over the interior of a hypercube in N dimensions.

The N-dimensional hypercube is the set of M points \( {\mathbf X}_1, {\mathbf X}_2, \cdots, {\mathbf X}_M \) such that:
\( {\mathbf X}_{ij} \in [lowerBound(j), upperBound(j)], \) for each dimension j, where the \( lowerBound(j) \) is the lower bound and \( upperBound(j) \) is the upperbound of the j-th coordinate.
