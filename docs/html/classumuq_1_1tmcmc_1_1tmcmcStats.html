Find the optimum value of \( p_j \).

The choice of \( p_j : j=1,\cdots,mâˆ’ 1 \) is essential. It is desirable to increase the p values slowly so that the transition between adjacent PDFs is smooth, but if the increase of the p values is too slow, the required number of intermediate stages will be huge. More intermediate stages mean more computational cost. \( p_{j+1} \) should be chosen so that the coefficient of variation tolCOV of the plausibility weights is equal to a prescribed threshold.
