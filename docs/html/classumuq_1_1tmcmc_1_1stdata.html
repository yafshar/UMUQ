◆ bbeta

double umuq::tmcmc::stdata::bbeta

\( \beta, \) a user-specified scaling factor in the TMCMC algorithm.
The proposal PDF for the MCMC step is a Gaussian distribution centered at the sample with covariance equal to \( \beta^2 COV(\Theta(j)), \) where \( \beta, \) is a user-specified scaling factor, and \( \Theta(j) \) is the collective samples from MCMC step.

◆ coefVarPresetThreshold

double umuq::tmcmc::stdata::coefVarPresetThreshold

A preset threshold for coefficient of variation of the plausibility of weights.
At each stage \( j, \) of the MCMC algorithm, \( \zeta_{j+1} \) is chosen such that the coefficient of variation of \( w_{j,k} \) is smaller than some preset threshold.

Reference:
Wu S, et. al. "Bayesian Annealed Sequential Importance Sampling: An Unbiased Version of Transitional Markov Chain Monte Carlo." ASME J. Risk Uncertainty Part B. 2017;4(1)
