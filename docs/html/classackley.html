Ackley function.

The Ackley function has several local minima, in 2-dimensional form it is characterized by a nearly flat outer region, and a large hole at the centre. It poses a risk for optimization algorithms, to be trapped in one of its many local minima. \( f(x) = -a e^{\left(-b \sqrt{\frac{1}{d}\sum_{i=1}^{d} x_i^2} \right)} - e^{\left(\frac{1}{d}\sum_{i=1}^{d} {\cos (cx_i)}\right)} + a + e^1 \)

Recommended variable values are: \( a = 20, b = 0.2 \text{and} c = 2\pi . \)
