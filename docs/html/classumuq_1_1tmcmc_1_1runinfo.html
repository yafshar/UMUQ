◆ logselection

std::vector<double> umuq::tmcmc::runinfo::logselection

In the Bayesian framework \( p(\theta|D)=\frac{p(D|\theta)p(\theta)}{p(D)}, \) where \( p(D) \) is the evidence and it can be estimated as a by-product in the TMCMC method.
logselection at each stage is used to estimate the log-evidence.
The log-evidence is estimated as a by-product of the method from \( \ln p(D) \approx \ln{\prod_{j=1}^{m-1}\left( \frac{1}{N_j} \sum_{k=1}^{N_j} w_{j,k} \right )}. \)
